Booking Policies

Attendants
Every machine rental includes 1–2 trained attendants responsible for operating equipment, serving guests, monitoring supplies, and maintaining cleanliness during the event.

Supplies Included
All rentals include the necessary supplies required to operate each machine such as popcorn kernels, cotton candy sugar, snow cone syrup, serving cups, bags, napkins, and other materials depending on the machine rented.

Deposit Requirement
A 25% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking. Your event date is not reserved until the deposit is received.

Cancellation Policy
All cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the scheduled event time.

Event Duration
Standard bookings include up to 4 hours of service. Additional hours will require an added hourly rate.

Final Payment
The remaining balance must be paid on the day of the event prior to the start of service.

Event Location
The event location must be provided at least 48 hours prior to the event.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Zelle
  • Apple Pay
  • Cash
